School Facilities

  • Library: Our school library is equipped with a wide range of books, reference materials, and digital resources to support students' learning.
  • Computer Lab: We have a state-of-the-art computer lab with high-speed internet access and the latest software for students to enhance their technological skills.
  • Science Lab: Our fully equipped science lab allows students to engage in hands-on experiments and explore various scientific concepts.
  • Sports Facilities: We offer a variety of sports facilities including a basketball court, football field, and indoor gymnasium to promote physical fitness and teamwork.
  • Art Studio: Our art studio provides students with the space and resources to unleash their creativity through painting, drawing, sculpture, and more.
  • Auditorium: Our school auditorium hosts various events, performances, and presentations, providing students with a platform to showcase their talents.
  • Cafeteria: The school cafeteria offers nutritious and delicious meals for students and staff, fostering a healthy eating environment.
  • Outdoor Spaces: We have well-maintained outdoor spaces including gardens and seating areas, providing students with opportunities for relaxation and outdoor learning.
