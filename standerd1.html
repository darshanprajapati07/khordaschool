Details

  • In Standard 1, we focus on laying the foundation for a child's education journey. Our curriculum is designed to introduce fundamental concepts in subjects like Mathematics, Language Arts, Science, and Social Studies in a fun and interactive way.
  • Students in Standard 1 will develop basic reading, writing, and numeracy skills. They will also learn about basic scientific concepts through hands-on activities and explore their immediate environment in Social Studies.
  • Our dedicated team of teachers ensures that each child receives personalized attention and support to help them transition smoothly into formal schooling. We also emphasize character development, creativity, and social skills to foster well-rounded individuals.
